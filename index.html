The Meadows Resort & Spa

The Meadows is an expansive garden resort. The Meadows is a cottage resort - quiet, comfortable and elegant. One of India's first eco-sensitive hotels, its gardens are home to over 50 species of birds and many varieties of trees and flowers. The Meadows is close to the important tourist sites of Ellora, Daulatabad, Ghrishneshwar and Bibi ka Maqbara, as also the railway station and local bazaar.

Amidst the gardens and shaded pathways lie 36 Deluxe and 8 Superior cottage style rooms, and 4 themed Suites. Each room has its own patio and lawn with picture windows offering views of the gardens or surrounding hills. Suites are independently sited to promote privacy and escape.
