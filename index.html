Discover A S Club

Your Premium Oasis in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar

Located perfectly at the 6 km stone on Nagar Road near Chordia Petrol Pump, Hotel A S Club provides an exceptional retreat for both corporate travelers and families.

Featuring 52 meticulously maintained air-conditioned rooms, our property is designed to be your home away from home. Unwind after a long day in our massive swimming pool, hit the gym, or enjoy a game of billiards before dining at our multi-cuisine restaurant.
