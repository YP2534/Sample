Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How far are you from the airport and railway station?


We are conveniently located 16 km from Chikalthana Airport and 5.5 km from the Railway Station. We also offer pick-up and drop services upon request.

Do you provide Wi-Fi in the rooms?


Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all cottages, suites, and common areas of the resort.

Are pets allowed at the resort?


While we love animals, to maintain the tranquility and botanical integrity of our gardens, pets are currently not allowed on the property.
